Greetings. My name is Navin Singh. I am a local storm watcher and local storm chaser that loves to watch storms and tropical cyclones around St. Petersburg, Tampa Bay, Florida.



2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, and 2022 all had tropical cyclone impacts on Tampa Bay.
2020, 2021, and 2022 featured 3 consective years of hurricane and storm surge impacts on Tampa Bay. 2020- Hurricane Eta brought wind and surge. 2021- Hurricane Elsa brought wind and surge. 2022- Hurricanes Ian and Nicole brought damaging winds and negative storm surge.

Years and Cyclone Impacts:

1995, 1996, 1998, 1999, 2000, 2001, 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022 had cyclone impacts in Tampa Bay. These impacts ranged from outer bands with brief squally conditions to core of a hurricane with damaging wind and storm surge.



Looking Ahead to the 2023 Atlantic Hurricane Season: Clusters to keep shifting eastward- Bahamas/SE FL/East Coast of FL/Carolinas/Georgia/Midatlantic.

Tampa Bay Weather & Tropical Cyclones

Active Hurricane Seasons for Tampa Bay

2022 Atlantic Hurricane Season

2022 was a very active hurricane season for the Florida Peninsula and Tampa Bay.
Hurricanes Ian and Nicole brought hurricane conditions across the area.

Hurricane Ian

Hurricane Ian Peaked with winds of 155 mph just prior to landfall.

  • Moderate Rain
    1. Squalls
    2. Core Moderate Training Rain
  • Extreme Wind
  • Extreme surge destruction and torrential rain South of the Bay in SW Florida

Hurricane Nicole

Hurricane Nicole crossed the Florida Peninsula with winds of 75 mph.

  1. Heavy Consistent Training Rain
  2. Strong Damaging Winds
  3. Catastrophic damage from surge and waves- east coast Florida Daytona Beach Area
